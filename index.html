    Description

    Geography app to learn world countries in a fun way.
    Your mission, should you decide to accept it, is to validate all countries.
    A country is validated as soon as you found it two times in only one try.
    Included are the following countries: All 193 members of the UN, Vatican City, 6 partially recognized states (Palestine, Western Sahara, Kosovo, Taiwan, Cook Islands, Niue), Antarctica, and 13 dependent regions (Greenland, American Samoa, Guam, Puerto Rico, French Guiana, French Polynesia, Guadeloupe, Mayotte, Martinique, New Caledonia, Réunion, Wallis and Futuna, Tokelau) that are so big and so near to other countries, that they can’t be omitted.
